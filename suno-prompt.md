## Step 1: Go to https://suno.com/create

## Step 2: Click "Custom Mode"

```
[Verse 1]

[Chorus]

[Chorus]
NASRED Twenty-Twenty-Six!
Religion! Education! Health!

[Outro]
NASRED 2026!

```

## Step 4: In "Style of Music" paste:

```
African inspirational, uplifting gospel choir, kora, talking drum, balafon, professional conference anthem, celebratory, 95 BPM, major key, Nigerian sound
```

## Step 5: Title it:

```
NASRED 2026 Conference Jingle
```

## Step 6: Click "Create"

---

**What happens next:**
- Suno will generate 2 versions of your jingle (about 2 minutes)
- Listen to both versions
- Click the three dots (⋮) on your favorite → Download → Audio
- You get an MP3 file ready to share!

**Free plan:** 50 credits/day = 10 songs per day (plenty for this project)

**Tip:** If you want variations, change the style to:
- "African highlife, upbeat, celebratory"
- "Gospel choir, inspirational, powerful"
- "Traditional Nigerian, juju music, festive"
