## PRODUCTION NOTES:

### Music Style:
- Blend of traditional African instruments (kora, talking drum, balafon)
- Modern orchestral elements for professionalism
- Uplifting, inspirational tone
- Tempo: Moderate (90-100 BPM)

### Voice Requirements:
- 3-4 voices (mix of male/female)
- Clear, professional Nigerian English
- One distinguished narrator (older, authoritative)
- One energetic voice (younger, dynamic)
- Chorus: Group of 4-6 voices

### Usage:
- Radio advertisements
- Social media videos
- Conference opening/closing
- WhatsApp promotional broadcasts
- Website background audio
